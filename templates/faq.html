FAQ

What is this site?

Diddy-Donation is specifically for really small donations (1p/£0.01) that you give to a webpage if you find it interesting, useful or fun.

What is the point of such a small donation?

  • A 1p donation is such a small commitment that you don't need to justify it to yourself - just do it!
  • It's a great way to show your support for a website - Put your money where your mouth is!
  • We believe it's more likely for 1000 people to donate 1p than it is for 1 person to donate 10 pounds.
  • It's not just about the money - If a author has recieved 500 1p pledges they will be encourgaged to continue what they are doing.
  • Life is a marathon, not a sprint. Websites can benefit from a tiny trickle of small donations because they all add up.

Why should I use diddydonation.com?

  • Free service - no fees. We don't take a slice of the pie.
  • Zero commitment - Donations are IOUs rather than confirmed, i.e you can make a whole bunch of pledges before you need to put your money where your mouth is. Actually, you're never committed to pay, unless you want to.
  • We are open and transparent. e.g. see our sourcecode here, and see our balance sheets here
  • You can pledge to any website, you don't have to wait for the website to set up a donations box. Until they have given details on how to get funds to their bank, your dontations can only be IOUs.

How do I give a larger donation?

There are plenty of other donations websites:

  • Pledgie - Free except for Paypal fees
  • Justgiving - 5% fee
  • Everyclick - 4.8% on Credit and Debit card donations.
  • Chipin
  • Twitpay
